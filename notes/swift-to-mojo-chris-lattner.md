# swift to mojo chris lattner

Pragmatic Engineer podcast interview, 1:32:03. Chris Lattner covers: how LLVM was built at University of Illinois and grown through Apple by delivering incremental business value; how Swift was developed in secret over a year and a half before disclosure to Apple management; the naming mistake of Swift 1.0 and lessons about language stability; the founding of Modular and Mojo in response to AI hardware stack fragmentation; Mojo's design philosophy of putting compiler power into library form for explicit developer control; expert resistance to language change following S-curve adoption patterns; and how LLM-friendly language design reduces to readability rather than LLM-specific features.
