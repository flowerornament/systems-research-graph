# we dont know how to compute sussman 2011

Strange Loop 2011. Gerald Sussman argues that the field of computing does not yet have adequate models for the kinds of computation we actually want to do. Primary contribution relevant to murail: the **propagator model**, a dataflow abstraction where cells hold partial information and propagators push conclusions between cells, with full truth maintenance tracking the provenance of every conclusion.

Rusher cites this talk directly in [[stop-writing-dead-programs-jack-rusher-2022]] as essential background for understanding propagator networks.
